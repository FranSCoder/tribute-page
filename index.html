DARK SOULS

The videogame saga directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, carries out in a dark, void world crowded by undeads.

  • February 5th, 2009: Launch of the first game of the saga, Demon's Souls.
  • September 22nd, 2011: Launch of Dark Souls.
  • March 11st, 2014: Launch of Dark Souls II.
  • March 24th, 2016: Launch of Dark Souls III.
  • Additional Related Games:
    • March 26th, 2015: Launch of Bloodborne, a getaway from the main story.
    • November 12nd, 2020: Launch of Demon's Souls - Remake.
    • February 25th, 2022: Launch of Elden Ring.

You can find more information about the Dark Souls saga at Dark Souls Wikipedia Page.
